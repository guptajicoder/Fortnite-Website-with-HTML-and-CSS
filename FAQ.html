Fortnite FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Fortnite

Q: What is Fortnite?
Fortnite is a popular online multiplayer game developed by Epic Games, known for its battle royale mode where 100 players fight to be the last one standing.
Q: What platforms is Fortnite available on?
Fortnite is available on various platforms including PC, consoles (PlayStation, Xbox), and mobile devices (iOS and Android).
Q: Is Fortnite free to play?
Yes, Fortnite Battle Royale mode is free to play. However, there are in-game purchases for cosmetics and battle passes.
